Little Pizzeria Review

by Ludwig van Beethoven

Posted on January 18th, 2014 at 6:00 PM

Very fucking fast delivery! Two Thumbs up! Experience the modern mastery of acclaimed chef Yves Durif and his team, in an intimate little pizzeria. Durif's passion for his craft is evident in the details: luxe yet tasting textures, a synergy between ingredients, flavors and textures that emphasizes the whole dining experience.
